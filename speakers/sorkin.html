Andrew Ross Sorkin

November Speaker

Andrew Ross Sorkin is a journalist, author, and television host known for his reporting on finance, business leadership, and major corporate events. He is widely respected for explaining complex economic topics in a clear and direct way.

Sorkin has interviewed influential leaders from business and government and has written extensively about Wall Street, innovation, and the forces shaping the modern economy.
