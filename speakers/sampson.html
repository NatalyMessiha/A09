Scott Sampson

February Speaker

Scott Sampson is a Canadian paleontologist, evolutionary biologist, and science communicator. He has served in major museum and science leadership roles and is widely recognized for making science exciting and accessible to children and families.

Sampson has authored books, appeared in educational media, and spoken around the world about dinosaurs, ecosystems, and humanity’s relationship with nature. He encourages curiosity, exploration, and a deeper appreciation for the natural world.

His message connects science with everyday life and reminds audiences that learning about the past can help us build a better future.
