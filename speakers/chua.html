Amy Chua

January Speaker

Amy Chua is a writer, professor, and legal scholar known for her books on culture, education, politics, and global power. Her work often explores how family values, identity, and ambition shape individuals and societies.

Chua’s speaking engagements challenge audiences to think critically about success, competition, and the ways different cultures influence leadership and achievement.
