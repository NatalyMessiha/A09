David Brancaccio

October Speaker

David Brancaccio is an award-winning journalist known for his work in public radio and television. He is best known as the host of Marketplace Morning Report, where he explains business and economic issues in a way that is accessible and engaging.

Throughout his career, Brancaccio has covered topics such as financial markets, public policy, and global economics. His reporting helps audiences understand how major events affect everyday life.
